Main components:

	Database
		Contains all sessions from different activities, where they are currently stored... 
	
	Session
		Contains information regarding an activity performed during a given time

	Synchroniser
		Contains capacities to retrieve sessions info from different sources, update the Database, and conversely update from the Database towards different sources

	Analyser
		Take info from a session or group or session and produce new information (summary, resultats, advices)
